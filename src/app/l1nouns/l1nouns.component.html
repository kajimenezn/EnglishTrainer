Nouns

Countable Nouns

Feature Description Examples
Countable Nouns that can be counted individually. cat (singular), cats (plural)
Quantifiers Use with numbers and words like "many," "few." three apples, several books
Plural Forms Have a plural form to indicate more than one. dog/dogs, book/books

Uncountable Nouns

Feature Description Examples
Uncountable Nouns that cannot be counted individually. water, sand, milk
Quantifiers Use with expressions like "some," "much," "a little." some water, a lot of information
No Plural Forms Do not have a plural form. furniture (not furnitures), advice
