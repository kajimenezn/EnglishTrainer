Subject Positive Form Negative Form

You You're (You are) You're not (You are not)
He He's (He is) He's not (He is not)
She She's (She is) She's not (She is not)

We We're (We are) We're not (We are not)
They They're (They are) They're not (They are not)

Note: "They're" can also be a contraction for "they were" in past tense.
