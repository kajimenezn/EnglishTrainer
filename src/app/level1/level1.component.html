English Trainer - Basic Level

Level 1: Greetings

Vocabulary:

  • Hello / Hi
  • Goodbye / Bye
  • Good morning
  • Good afternoon
  • Good evening
  • My name is...
  • What's your name?
  • Nice to meet you

Conversations:

Dialogue 1:

A: Hello! My name is John.
B: Hi, John! My name is Mary.
A: Nice to meet you, Mary.
B: Nice to meet you too, John.

Dialogue 2:

A: Good morning!
B: Good morning! How are you?
A: I'm fine, thank you. And you?
B: I'm good, thanks.

Activities:

  • Role Play: Los estudiantes se turnan para presentarse unos a otros utilizando el vocabulario aprendido.
  • Matching Exercise: Emparejar saludos con las respuestas correctas.
  • Fill in the Blanks: Completar diálogos con las palabras correctas.
